## What are Subscription Plans?

GadgetCloud offers different subscription plans to match your needs, whether you're managing a few personal devices or an entire family's gadgets. Each plan comes with:

- **Device Limit**: Maximum number of devices you can track
- **Storage Space**: How much space you have for photos, receipts, and documents
- **Features**: Additional capabilities like family sharing and premium support

All existing users are on our **Standard Plan** (free forever!) and can upgrade anytime for more capacity and features.

## Viewing Your Subscription

### Step 1: Navigate to Your Profile

1. Click on your **profile icon** in the top-right corner

### Step 2: Go to Subscription Tab

1. In your profile page, click on the **"Subscription"** tab

## Understanding Your Usage

### Device Count

Every gadget you add to GadgetCloud counts toward your device limit:

- Tablets

- Any other tracked item

### Storage Space

Your storage is used for:

## What Happens at Limits

### When You Reach Your Device Limit

**Scenario**: You're on Standard plan (5 devices) and try to add a 6th device.

**What Happens**:
1. You'll see an error message: **"Device limit exceeded"**
2. The message explains: "Your Standard Plan allows 5 devices. You currently have 5. Upgrade your plan to add more devices."
3. An **"Upgrade Plan"** button appears in the error message
4. The device is **not added** until you upgrade or remove an existing device

**Your Options**:
- **Option 1**: Upgrade to Family (10 devices) or Premium (unlimited)
- **Option 2**: Delete an old device you no longer need
- **Option 3**: Archive a device temporarily (coming soon)

### When You Reach Your Storage Limit

**Scenario**: You're on Standard plan (100 MB) and try to upload a file that would exceed your limit.

**What Happens**:
1. You'll see an error message: **"Storage limit exceeded"**
2. The message shows:
   - Your current usage (e.g., 95 MB)
   - File size you're trying to upload (e.g., 10 MB)
   - Your limit (100 MB)
3. An **"Upgrade Plan"** button appears in the error message
4. The file is **not uploaded** until you upgrade or free up space

**Your Options**:
- **Option 1**: Upgrade to Family (500 MB) or Premium (5 GB)
- **Option 2**: Delete old documents you don't need
- **Option 3**: Download and then delete large files to free space

### Pro Tips for Managing Limits

**Device Limit Tips**:
- Review your devices regularly and remove ones you've sold or no longer own
- Combine similar devices (e.g., multiple old phones ‚Üí keep only current phone)
- Archive rarely-used devices instead of tracking them actively

**Storage Limit Tips**:
- Delete duplicate photos or receipts
- Keep only essential documents (warranty papers, important receipts)
- Download large files (like manuals) before deleting to keep a local copy
- Compress images before uploading (use "Medium" quality instead of "High")
- Upload only clear, readable receipts (delete blurry photos)

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: How much does GadgetCloud cost?**
A: Our Standard plan is free forever. Family plan is ‚Çπ999/month and Premium is ‚Çπ2,999/month.

**Q: Can I try a paid plan for free?**
A: Currently, we don't offer free trials, but you can upgrade and downgrade anytime. Payment is monthly, so you're not locked in.

**Q: Is there an annual plan with a discount?**
A: Not yet, but we're considering it! Annual plans with discounts may be available in the future.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, UPI, and net banking (payment integration coming soon).

### Plan Management

**Q: Can I change my plan anytime?**
A: Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade anytime from your Subscription tab.

**Q: What happens if I downgrade?**
A: You'll keep access to your existing data, but you won't be able to add new devices or upload documents if you're over the new limit. You'll need to remove devices or files to get back under the limit.

**Q: Will I lose my data if I downgrade?**
A: No, you never lose your data. Your existing devices and documents remain accessible even if you're over the new plan's limits. You just can't add more until you're under the limit.

**Q: How do I cancel my subscription?**
A: You can downgrade to the free Standard plan anytime. Your account remains active, and you keep all your data (subject to limits).

**Q: Do you offer refunds?**
A: Refunds are handled case-by-case. Please contact support at support@gadgetcloud.io to discuss your situation.

### Limits & Usage

**Q: What counts as a "device"?**
A: Any gadget you add to GadgetCloud counts as one device: phones, laptops, tablets, watches, gaming consoles, cameras, appliances, etc.

**Q: Can I temporarily exceed my limits?**
A: No, limits are enforced in real-time. You must upgrade or remove items to continue adding more.

**Q: What file types count toward storage?**
A: All uploaded files count: photos (JPG, PNG), documents (PDF), and any other attachments.

**Q: How is storage calculated?**
A: Storage is the total size of all files you've uploaded across all devices. We show this in MB (megabytes) or GB (gigabytes) on your Subscription tab.

**Q: Do deleted files free up storage immediately?**
A: Yes, when you delete a document, that storage is immediately available for new uploads.

**Q: Can I see which files use the most storage?**
A: Not yet, but we're working on a storage breakdown view that shows file sizes. Coming soon!

### Family Sharing

**Q: How does family sharing work?**
A: Family sharing (available on Family and Premium plans) lets you invite up to 5 family members to view and manage shared devices. Each member has their own login.

**Q: Do family members count toward my device limit?**
A: Yes, all devices added by any family member count toward the plan's total device limit (10 for Family, unlimited for Premium).

**Q: Can I remove family members?**
A: Yes, the plan owner can add or remove family members anytime from the Family Sharing settings.

**Q: Do family members have their own storage?**
A: No, storage is shared across all family members. The total storage for all members cannot exceed the plan limit (500 MB for Family, 5 GB for Premium).

### Features & Support

**Q: What is AI photo recognition?**
A: When you upload a photo of your device (like a phone's back label), our AI automatically extracts the brand, model, and serial number to save you time.

**Q: How fast is support response?**
A:
- Standard: 48 hours
- Family: 24 hours (priority)
- Premium: 12 hours (highest priority)

**Q: What support channels are available?**
A:
- Standard: Email & in-app messages
- Family: Email, in-app, & phone
- Premium: Email, in-app, phone, & dedicated account manager

**Q: What is a dedicated account manager?**
A: Premium users get a dedicated account manager who knows your account, proactively helps with issues, and provides personalized support.

**Q: What's the difference between basic and advanced analytics?**
A:
- Basic (Standard): Device counts, warranty expiration tracking
- Advanced (Family/Premium): Detailed insights, spending trends, device depreciation, category breakdowns, custom reports

### Technical Questions

**Q: Will my devices sync across plans?**
A: Yes, all your devices and data are always synced, regardless of which plan you're on.

**Q: What happens to my documents if I exceed the limit?**
A: You can still view and download existing documents, but you cannot upload new ones until you upgrade or free up space.

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: Yes, you can export your devices and documents anytime from the Settings page (export feature coming soon).

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Absolutely. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We follow industry-standard security practices. Read our [Security Policy](#) for details.

### Upgrading & Billing

**Q: When am I charged after upgrading?**
A: Billing starts immediately upon upgrade, and you're charged monthly from that date.

**Q: Can I upgrade in the middle of the month?**
A: Yes, you can upgrade anytime. You'll be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of the current month.

**Q: What happens if my payment fails?**
A: We'll notify you by email and give you a grace period to update your payment method. During this time, your plan remains active. If payment isn't received within 7 days, you'll be downgraded to Standard.

**Q: Can I get an invoice for my subscription?**
A: Yes, invoices are automatically emailed after each payment. You can also download past invoices from your Billing page.

**Q: Do you support GST invoices for businesses?**
A: Yes, if you provide your GSTIN, we'll issue GST-compliant invoices.
