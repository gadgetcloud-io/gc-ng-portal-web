## ğŸ”„ Plan Changes

### Can I change my plan anytime?
Yes! Upgrade or downgrade instantly from your Subscription tab in your profile.

### What happens if I downgrade?
- You keep all existing data
- Can't add new devices/documents if over new limit
- Must remove items to get back under limit

### Will I lose data if I downgrade?
Never! All devices and documents remain accessible, but you can't add more until you're under the new limit.

### How do I cancel?
Downgrade to the free Standard plan. Your account and data remain active.

## ğŸ“Š Limits & Usage

### What counts as a "device"?
Any gadget you add: phones, laptops, tablets, watches, consoles, cameras, appliances, etc.

### Can I exceed limits temporarily?
No. Limits are enforced in real-time. Upgrade or remove items to continue.

### How is storage calculated?
Total size of all files uploaded across all devices (photos, PDFs, documents).

### What happens when I hit my device limit?
- Error message appears
- Cannot add more devices
- **Options**: Upgrade, or delete old devices

### What happens when storage is full?
- Cannot upload new files
- Existing files remain accessible
- **Options**: Upgrade, or delete old files

### Do deleted files free up storage immediately?
Yes! Storage is available right away.

### How does family sharing work?
Invite up to 5 family members. Each has their own login. All can view/manage shared devices.

### Who pays for the subscription?
The plan owner pays. Family members get access included.

### Do family members count toward device limit?
Yes. All devices from all members count toward the plan's total (10 for Family, unlimited for Premium).

### Is storage shared?
Yes. Total storage for all family members cannot exceed the plan limit.

### Can I remove family members?
Yes, anytime from Family Sharing settings.
